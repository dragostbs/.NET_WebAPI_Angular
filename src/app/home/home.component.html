Fundamental Analysis

  • Fundamental analysis evaluates stocks by attempting to measure their intrinsic value.
  • The main focus is the overall economy and industry conditions to the financial strength and management of companies.
  • Earnings, expenses, assets, and liabilities all come under scrutiny by fundamental analysts.

Technical Analysis

  • Technical analysis evaluates statistical trends, such as movements in a stock's price and volume.
  • The main focus is the stock charts to identify patterns and trends that suggest what a stock will do in the future.
  • Multiple indicators are used to identify patterns and trends in the stock charts.
